Sri Lankan A/L Physical Science Stream


Subjects in the Physical Science Stream


The Physical Science stream in Sri Lanka is a prominent academic pathway offered at the Advanced Level (A/L) in secondary education, designed for students with a strong interest in mathematics, physics, and chemistry. The core subjects in this stream include:

  • Combined Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

The Physical Science stream is ideal for students aiming to pursue careers in engineering, physical sciences, computer science, and other related fields. It provides a rigorous foundation in mathematical and scientific principles, essential for understanding the physical world and solving complex problems in technology and engineering.

This stream emphasizes analytical thinking, problem-solving, and the application of scientific methods. By studying subjects such as Combined Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry, students gain a deep understanding of the laws of nature, the behavior of matter, and the mathematical frameworks that describe physical phenomena. The inclusion of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) offers additional skills in computing and digital technologies, which are increasingly important in modern science and engineering.

The Physical Science stream is an excellent choice for students with a passion for mathematics and the physical sciences, offering numerous opportunities for further education and professional development in these challenging and rewarding fields.

The Physical Science stream in Sri Lanka offers a broad spectrum of academic programs that emphasize the study of physical sciences, engineering, and technology. This stream caters to students interested in careers in engineering, computer science, and applied physical sciences. Admission to the programs listed above is typically based on merit, with students being selected according to their academic performance and examination results. The Physical Science stream provides students with a comprehensive education in various scientific and technological disciplines, preparing them for careers in engineering, computer science, data analysis, and applied sciences.
