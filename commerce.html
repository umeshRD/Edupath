Sri Lankan A/L Commerce Stream


Subjects in the Commerce Stream

The Commerce stream in Sri Lanka is a key academic pathway offered at the Advanced Level (A/L) in secondary education, designed for students interested in the business and economic sectors. The core subjects in this stream include:

  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Accounting

Students may choose to study at least two of the core subjects mentioned above. The third subject can be selected from the following options:

  • Agricultural Science
  • Geography
  • Business Statistics
  • German
  • Combined Mathematics or Mathematics
  • History
  • Political Science
  • English
  • Logic & Scientific Method
  • French
  • Information & Communication Technology

The Commerce stream is tailored for students aiming to pursue careers in business management, finance, accounting, economics, and entrepreneurship. It offers a solid foundation in understanding business principles, economic theories, and financial practices, which are essential for navigating the corporate world and contributing to the economy.

This stream fosters analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a deep understanding of the commercial environment. By studying subjects such as Economics and Business Studies, students gain insights into market dynamics, organizational behavior, and the financial systems that drive economies. These skills are highly valued in various professional fields, including banking, corporate management, government services, and international trade.

The Commerce stream is an excellent choice for students with a passion for business and economics, offering numerous opportunities for further education and professional growth in these fields.

Admission and Academic Focus

Admission to the programs listed above is generally based on merit, with students selected according to their academic performance and examination results. The Commerce stream provides students with a solid foundation in various business disciplines, promoting skills in management, accounting, finance, and information systems.

By choosing from a range of programs and specializations, students can tailor their education to their career aspirations, whether in management, finance, real estate, or business information systems. The Commerce stream prepares students for a wide array of career opportunities in both public and private sectors, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the modern business world.
